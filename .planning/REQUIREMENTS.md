# Requirements

## Scope Summary

V1 is a public knowledge platform for the startup incubator and accelerator industry. It must help external stakeholders discover, read, and understand authoritative terminology and related intelligence, while supporting UBI Global's thought-leadership and lead-generation goals.

## V1 Requirements

### Discovery

- `DISC-01` Users can run a site-wide search across glossary entries, KPI/metric pages, and reports/publications from a single search surface.
- `DISC-02` Search results clearly indicate content type, title, short context, and destination so users can quickly choose the right result.
- `DISC-03` Users can browse content by logical topic groupings or sections when they do not know the exact term to search for.

### Content Experience

- `CONT-01` Users can open a glossary term page and read a clear definition, context, and related industry meaning in a readable layout.
- `CONT-02` Users can open a KPI or metric page and understand what the term means and why it matters in industry context.
- `CONT-03` Users can open a report or publication page and access summary information plus the primary destination for the report content or download.
- `CONT-04` Public content pages show freshness indicators such as updated dates when available so users can judge recency and authority.

### Information Architecture

- `IA-01` The product has a clear public navigation model that helps users move between glossary, KPIs/metrics, and publications/reports.
- `IA-02` The reading experience works well on desktop and mobile and prioritizes legibility over dense interface chrome.
- `IA-03` Public content pages are indexable and shareable so Compass can grow thought leadership through organic discovery.

### Lead Generation

- `LEAD-01` Compass includes clear, non-intrusive calls to action that turn engaged readers into qualified leads for UBI Global.
- `LEAD-02` Lead-generation surfaces align with the content context, for example report-related CTAs near publication content rather than generic interruptions everywhere.

### Platform

- `PLAT-01` V1 is fully open with no account creation, login wall, or member-only gating for core Compass content.
- `PLAT-02` The system supports structured public content across at least glossary, KPI/metric, and report/publication content types.
- `PLAT-04` The editorial workflow supports publishable images and embedded video without forcing editors to manage GitBook as the canonical source.
- `PLAT-03` The product captures baseline analytics needed to understand search usage, content engagement, and lead-generation performance.

## V2 / Deferred

- Saved items or reading lists
- Compare concepts or metrics side by side
- Member-only or gated content experiences
- Natural-language assistant or conversational search
- Advanced editorial workflow and approvals
- Personalized recommendations

## Out of Scope

- Benchmarking dashboards for end users
- Private member portal capabilities
- Community or collaboration features
