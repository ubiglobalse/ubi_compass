# Roadmap

## Overview

Compass v1 should launch as a public, searchable industry-intelligence destination that is easy to read, easy to discover, and aligned with UBI Global's thought-leadership and lead-generation goals. The roadmap is organized around user-visible outcomes rather than technical layers, so each phase delivers a coherent capability.

## Phases

### Phase 1 - Public Foundation

**Goal:** Compass has a stable GitBook publishing foundation with clear information architecture, structured Markdown content, and a workable media pattern for images and video.

**Dependencies:** None

**Requirements:**
- `PLAT-01`
- `PLAT-02`
- `IA-01`

**Success Criteria:**
1. Users can access Compass freely without creating an account or encountering membership gates.
2. The product exposes a clear top-level structure for glossary, KPI/metric, and report/publication content.
3. The system can represent the required public content types in a structured way that supports future growth.
4. Editors have a clear repo-based workflow for managing publishable text, images, and embedded video before content reaches GitBook.

### Phase 2 - Discovery and Navigation

**Goal:** Users can find relevant Compass content whether they know an exact term or are exploring the subject area more broadly.

**Dependencies:** Phase 1

**Requirements:**
- `DISC-01`
- `DISC-02`
- `DISC-03`

**Success Criteria:**
1. Users can search once and receive results from all supported public content types.
2. Search results provide enough context for users to distinguish between glossary, KPI/metric, and report content.
3. Users who do not know the exact term can still browse by section or topic and reach useful content.

### Phase 3 - Readable Intelligence Experience

**Goal:** Users can read Compass content comfortably and understand the industry meaning of terms, metrics, and publication material.

**Dependencies:** Phase 1

**Requirements:**
- `CONT-01`
- `CONT-02`
- `CONT-03`
- `CONT-04`
- `IA-02`

**Success Criteria:**
1. Users can open glossary terms and understand definitions and context in a clean reading experience.
2. Users can open KPI or metric content and understand what a metric means and why it matters.
3. Users can open report or publication entries and access summary information plus the primary destination for the full resource.
4. Users can judge the freshness and authority of content through visible recency signals where available.
5. Reading experiences remain usable and legible across desktop and mobile.

### Phase 4 - Public Reach and Lead Capture

**Goal:** Compass turns public content consumption into broader reach, stronger thought leadership, and qualified top-of-funnel lead capture.

**Dependencies:** Phase 2, Phase 3

**Requirements:**
- `IA-03`
- `LEAD-01`
- `LEAD-02`
- `PLAT-03`

**Success Criteria:**
1. Public Compass pages are indexable, shareable, and positioned to earn organic discovery.
2. Readers encounter context-aware calls to action that feel relevant to the content they are consuming.
3. UBI Global can measure baseline usage for search, content engagement, and lead-generation conversion points.
4. The public experience supports thought leadership without sacrificing readability or trust.
