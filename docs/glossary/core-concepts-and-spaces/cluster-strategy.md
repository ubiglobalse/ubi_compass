# Cluster Strategy

## Summary

Cluster strategy is a place-based approach in which companies, startups, institutions, and support organizations in the same sector or region work together to build shared competitive advantage.

## Definition

The strategy brings together participants such as startups, universities, corporations, investors, and public institutions around a defined industry or geography. By concentrating talent, infrastructure, and relationships, cluster strategies create conditions that make collaboration and innovation easier to sustain.

## Why It Matters

Clusters can strengthen regional growth by improving knowledge exchange, attracting investment, and helping participants solve common challenges together. They also make it easier for specialized support organizations to serve a concentrated community of innovators.

## Industry Context

Well-known clusters such as Silicon Valley or major life-sciences hubs show how trusted networks, deep expertise, and support infrastructure can reinforce one another over time. In startup support ecosystems, cluster strategy often intersects with policy, ecosystem building, and sector-focused program design.
