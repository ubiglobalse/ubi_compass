# Community Impact

## Summary

Community impact refers to the social benefits a program creates through its engagement with the wider community around it.

## Definition

In Compass, this term measures the outputs and outcomes of initiatives that improve social well-being, inclusion, cohesion, or broader ecosystem value beyond direct startup performance alone.

## Why It Matters

Startup support programs often influence far more than company growth. Community impact helps capture their contribution to local well-being, public trust, and the long-term health of the surrounding ecosystem.

## Industry Context

Community impact is closely related to ESG thinking because it highlights outcomes that are social rather than purely financial. It can strengthen program reputation and stakeholder support, but it is often harder to measure and attribute than more direct business metrics.
