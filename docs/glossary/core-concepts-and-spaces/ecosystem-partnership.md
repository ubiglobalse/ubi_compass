# Ecosystem Partnership

## Summary

An ecosystem partnership is a collaborative relationship among organizations that work together to create shared value inside a broader innovation ecosystem.

## Definition

These partnerships connect businesses, institutions, and support actors that combine complementary strengths, resources, or reach. They are often designed to support innovation, market access, shared learning, or system-level problem-solving.

## Why It Matters

No single organization can provide every capability a healthy ecosystem needs. Partnerships allow participants to reach new users, share risk, access specialized expertise, and expand the overall value available to founders and other stakeholders.

## Industry Context

Ecosystem partnerships may involve co-innovation, co-investment, shared programming, joint market activity, or cross-sector collaboration. Their value depends on trust, aligned incentives, and a clear understanding of how each partner contributes to the broader system.
