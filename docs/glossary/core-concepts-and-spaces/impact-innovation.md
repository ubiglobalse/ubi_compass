# Impact Innovation

## Summary

Impact innovation is innovation designed to create commercial value while also delivering meaningful social, environmental, or community benefit.

## Definition

The concept refers to products, services, business models, or ecosystem approaches that combine market viability with positive long-term outcomes beyond profit alone. It typically connects innovation activity with goals such as sustainability, equity, resilience, or public benefit.

## Why It Matters

Impact innovation helps organizations address real societal challenges without separating mission from business performance. It also creates a shared language for founders, investors, and support organizations that want innovation to contribute to broader progress as well as financial return.

## Industry Context

This approach is increasingly visible in ecosystems focused on climate, inclusion, health, and sustainable development. Programs that support impact innovation often emphasize measurable outcomes, stakeholder trust, and alignment between mission-driven goals and scalable execution.
