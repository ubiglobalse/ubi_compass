# Extended Reality (XR)

## Summary

Extended reality, or XR, is an umbrella term that includes virtual reality, augmented reality, and mixed reality technologies.

## Definition

XR refers to immersive or blended digital experiences delivered through devices such as headsets, smart glasses, and mobile hardware. These systems can overlay digital information onto the physical world or create fully simulated environments.

## Why It Matters

XR opens new possibilities for training, design, collaboration, simulation, retail, healthcare, and entertainment. For startups, it can create differentiated product experiences; for incubators, it represents a growing area of technical specialization and ecosystem support.

## Industry Context

Programs supporting XR ventures often help founders with prototyping, user experience, hardware-software integration, and sector-specific adoption challenges. XR also overlaps with broader digital-wave trends such as simulation, immersive interaction, and human-machine collaboration.
