# Climate Strategy

## Summary

Climate strategy refers to whether an organization has a formal plan for addressing environmental impact and embedding climate action into program operations.

## Definition

In Compass, this term measures the extent to which climate-related goals are integrated into organizational planning and everyday practice. A climate strategy can include priorities around emissions, operations, resource use, environmental stewardship, and alignment with wider sustainability commitments.

## Why It Matters

Programs that treat climate action strategically are better positioned to translate environmental intent into durable operational choices. A clear strategy can strengthen accountability, guide resource allocation, and connect local program activity to broader sustainability goals.

## Industry Context

Climate strategy often overlaps with broader ESG work, but it keeps a specific focus on environmental performance. The maturity and scope of these strategies can vary widely across organizations depending on mission, sector focus, geography, and available capability.
