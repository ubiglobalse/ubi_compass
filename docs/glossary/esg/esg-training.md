# ESG Training

## Summary

ESG training refers to the workshops, learning sessions, or educational support a program provides to help startups build environmental, social, and governance capability.

## Definition

This term measures whether a program actively teaches ESG-related concepts and practices rather than expecting startups to develop that knowledge independently. Training can cover compliance, sustainability strategy, responsible leadership, DEI, or other ESG-related topics.

## Why It Matters

Training helps operationalize ESG by turning abstract expectations into practical founder capability. It supports responsible business development and makes it more likely that startups can act on ESG goals in a meaningful way.

## Industry Context

The scope and depth of ESG training vary significantly across programs. Some may offer occasional workshops, while others integrate ESG learning deeply into program delivery. In either case, training is a useful indicator that the program is investing in continuous ESG development.
