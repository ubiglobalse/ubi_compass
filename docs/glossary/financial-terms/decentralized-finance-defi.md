# Decentralized Finance (DeFi)

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) is an emerging financial technology ecosystem that leverages blockchain and cryptocurrencies to remove the need for intermediaries such as banks. By enabling peer-to-peer transactions and programmable smart contracts, DeFi democratizes access to capital and financial services, offering startups and investors more direct, transparent, and cost-effective alternatives.
### Did you know...
DeFi platforms provide diverse financial services, including lending, borrowing, trading, asset management, and insurance, all executed through decentralized applications (DApps) on blockchain networks. Startups can access capital directly from global investors without traditional gatekeepers, while investors benefit from new opportunities in a permissionless and transparent environment. Incubators support startups working in DeFi by providing technology expertise, regulatory guidance, and connections to blockchain networks.
### Bonus Fact
Forward-looking incubators prepare startups to navigate challenges by fostering innovation, compliance, and secure development practices within the DeFi ecosystem.
