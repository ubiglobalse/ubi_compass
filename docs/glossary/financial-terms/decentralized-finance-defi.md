# Decentralized Finance (DeFi)

## Summary

Decentralized finance, or DeFi, is a blockchain-based financial ecosystem that enables peer-to-peer financial activity without relying on traditional intermediaries like banks.

## Definition

DeFi uses smart contracts and decentralized applications to support services such as lending, borrowing, trading, insurance, and asset management. The model is designed to make financial services more open, programmable, and globally accessible.

## Why It Matters

For startups and innovators, DeFi creates new ways to access capital, build financial products, and experiment with alternative market structures. It also introduces new regulatory, security, and governance questions that incubators and accelerators may need to help founders navigate.

## Industry Context

DeFi sits at the intersection of finance, software, and cryptographic infrastructure. While it can lower barriers and create new forms of financial participation, it also requires careful attention to compliance, protocol risk, and secure product design.
