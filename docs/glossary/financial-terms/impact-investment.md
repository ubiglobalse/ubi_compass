# Impact Investment

## Summary

Impact investment is capital deployed with the intention of generating measurable social or environmental benefit alongside financial return.

## Definition

Unlike investing focused only on profit, impact investment evaluates both financial performance and the positive outcomes created by the business or project receiving capital. It is often used in sectors such as climate, health, education, inclusion, and community development.

## Why It Matters

Impact investment gives founders and innovation programs a financing pathway that better aligns mission-driven work with sustainable business growth. It also creates pressure for clearer measurement of outcomes rather than relying on good intentions alone.

## Industry Context

Impact investors often expect evidence about social or environmental performance in addition to business fundamentals. For incubators and accelerators, this makes impact measurement, ESG thinking, and outcome reporting more important for the ventures they support.
