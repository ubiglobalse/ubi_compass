# Ideation

## Summary

Ideation is the process of generating, exploring, and refining new ideas for products, services, or business opportunities.

## Definition

It includes activities such as brainstorming, concept development, idea testing, and early prioritization. The goal is to surface possibilities and shape them into directions worth validating further.

## Why It Matters

Ideation is often the starting point for innovation. It helps teams move from vague opportunity areas into more defined concepts that can later be tested, built, or commercialized.

## Industry Context

Incubators and innovation programs frequently use structured methods such as design thinking, workshops, and collaborative sessions to improve ideation quality. Strong ideation work can broaden perspectives while still creating enough discipline to decide what is worth pursuing.
