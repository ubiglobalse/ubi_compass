# Pitch Competition

## Summary

A pitch competition is an event where founders present their startup or business idea to judges in order to win funding, recognition, mentorship, or other forms of support.

## Definition

Participants usually deliver a short presentation that explains the problem, solution, business model, team, and traction behind their venture. Judges often evaluate startups on criteria such as innovation, scalability, readiness, and clarity of execution.

## Why It Matters

Pitch competitions can help founders improve storytelling, gain exposure, and access networks or resources beyond prize money alone. For ecosystems, they also create visible moments of startup discovery and community engagement.

## Industry Context

These competitions are common within universities, incubators, accelerators, and innovation events. Their value depends not only on the competition itself but also on the quality of follow-up support and the relevance of the audience involved.
