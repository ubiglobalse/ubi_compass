# Unicorn Startup

## Summary

A unicorn startup is a privately held company valued at $1 billion or more, typically based on investor expectations about future growth rather than current profitability.

## Definition

The term is used to describe rare startups that reach very high private valuations through funding rounds backed by venture capital, private equity, or other major investors. A related term, `decacorn`, is often used for startups valued at $10 billion or more.

## Why It Matters

Unicorn status is often treated as a signal of scale, ambition, and market influence. In startup ecosystems, unicorns shape investor attention, founder expectations, and narratives about what high-growth success can look like.

## Industry Context

Unicorns are commonly associated with technology-driven sectors, aggressive scaling, and strong access to capital. Their valuations can reflect real traction, but they can also be shaped by broader market sentiment and long-term growth assumptions.
