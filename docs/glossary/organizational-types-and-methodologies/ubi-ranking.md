# UBI Ranking

## Summary

The UBI Ranking compares startup incubators and accelerators against peer organizations with similar structures and contexts so they can understand how they perform relative to others.

## Definition

Organizations submit performance information that UBI Global evaluates across multiple areas, such as client outcomes, ecosystem impact, services, and organizational effectiveness. The ranking is designed to benchmark programs in a structured way rather than compare every organization under one undifferentiated standard.

## Why It Matters

Ranking creates external reference points that programs can use to understand strengths, identify gaps, and communicate credibility. For many incubators and accelerators, the ranking also serves as a visibility tool that can help attract founders, partners, and investors.

## Industry Context

In the broader UBI Global framework, ranking is connected to benchmarking and learning, not just recognition. The strongest use of a ranking system is as an improvement mechanism: programs can compare themselves with relevant peers, interpret their results, and make more informed strategic decisions about services, positioning, and ecosystem role.
