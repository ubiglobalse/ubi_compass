# Client Startups Accepted

## Summary

Client startups accepted measures how many startups are admitted into the program during a defined period.

## Definition

Quantifies the inflow of startup participants attracted to the program.

## Interpretation

This metric helps readers understand program demand and intake volume, but it should be read alongside selectivity, program capacity, and the quality or fit of accepted startups.

## Why It Matters

Attracting startups enriches the local entrepreneurial ecosystem and drives innovation.

## Advantages

- Simple to track and measure.
- Indicates program demand.

## Limitations

- Does not assess the quality or selectiveness of startups.
- A larger intake may dilute program resources.
