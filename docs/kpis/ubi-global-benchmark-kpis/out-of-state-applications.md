# Out-of-State Applications

## Summary

Number of applications received from startups outside the program's immediate geographic area.

## Definition

Measures national or international reach and prestige.

## Interpretation

Evaluates program attractiveness to a wider pool of entrepreneurs.

## Why It Matters

A strong reputation beyond local boundaries reflects program impact and brand strength.

## Advantages

- Reflects the program's ability to attract diverse talent.
- Indicates prestige and visibility.

## Limitations

- Influenced by marketing reach and regional policies.
- May not always correlate with local ecosystem benefits.
