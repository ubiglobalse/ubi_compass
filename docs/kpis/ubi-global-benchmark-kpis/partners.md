# Partners

## Summary

Partners measures the number of business development partners a program engages directly or helps connect to its startups.

## Definition

Measures success in connecting startups with external collaborators and ecosystem players.

## Interpretation

This metric can indicate how active the program is as a connector within the ecosystem, but the number should be read alongside the quality, relevance, and depth of those partnerships.

## Why It Matters

Strong partnerships can improve business opportunities and resource access.

## Advantages

- Reflects the program's role as a community builder.
- Encourages knowledge exchange and collaboration.

## Limitations

- Quantitative counts may obscure partnership quality.
- Regional and legal differences can affect partnering ability.
