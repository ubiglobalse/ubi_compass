# Sales Revenue

Total sales revenue generated by client startups and alumni as a result of the program's support during a given period.

## Definition

Measures the amount of sales generated by client startups through program activities and support.

## Purpose

Evaluates the program's effectiveness in driving financial growth and commercial success for startups.

## Rationale

Sales revenue is widely recognized as a direct indicator of startup viability and growth prospects.

## Advantages

- Provides clear, quantifiable financial data.
- Reflects the financial sustainability and market acceptance of supported startups.

## Limitations

- May be influenced by external economic conditions beyond program control.
- Not all programs prioritize revenue generation equally.
