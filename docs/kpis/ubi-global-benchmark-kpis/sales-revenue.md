# Sales Revenue

## Summary

Sales revenue measures the total revenue generated by client startups and alumni during a given period as an outcome associated with the program's support.

## Definition

Measures the amount of sales generated by client startups through program activities and support.

## Interpretation

Higher sales revenue usually signals that supported startups are finding customers, generating commercial traction, and building a more sustainable financial base.

## Why It Matters

Sales revenue is widely recognized as a direct indicator of startup viability, product-market traction, and growth potential. For programs that aim to strengthen company performance, it is one of the clearest outcome measures available.

## Advantages

- Provides clear, quantifiable financial data.
- Reflects the financial sustainability and market acceptance of supported startups.

## Limitations

- May be influenced by external economic conditions beyond program control.
- Not all programs prioritize revenue generation equally.
