# World Benchmark Report 2019 - 2020

## About
The UBI Global World Benchmark Report 19/20 presents the most comprehensive assessment of business incubators and accelerators worldwide, benchmarking 364 programs across 82 countries . Built on years of research and collaboration, the report reveals how innovation programs create measurable impact — driving entrepreneurship, economic growth, and sustainable development across ecosystems.
Through a data-driven evaluation framework, the report analyzes key performance indicators across ecosystem value, client startup success, and program excellence. It uncovers best practices, organizational models, and financial insights that distinguish top-performing incubators and accelerators, empowering readers to make informed, strategic decisions for growth and efficiency.
Designed for innovation leaders, universities, policymakers, and ecosystem builders, the World Benchmark Report 19/20 offers a powerful lens into global performance and trends. By connecting data with real-world impact, it serves as the go-to reference for organizations committed to shaping the future of innovation. 🌍✨
