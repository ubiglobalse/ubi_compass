# Best Practices at Top University-linked Business Incubators - Vol 1

## Summary

This first volume of UBI Global's university-linked incubator case studies presents 12 international examples of top-performing programs and the practices behind their strong results.

The report focuses on incubators and accelerators that support startup creation, build sustainable entrepreneurship hubs, and contribute meaningfully to their surrounding innovation ecosystems.
