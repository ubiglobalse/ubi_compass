# Best Practices at Top University-linked Business Incubators - Vol 1

## About
12 International Case Studies of Top-Performing Business Incubators
University-linked business incubators and accelerators are key players in many countries’ innovation strategies. Designed to:
- support the creation of successful companies,
- mature into self-sustaining entrepreneurship hubs, and
- catalyze the development of their respective innovation ecosystems.
This first volume of our 2018 Best Practices at University-Linked Business Incubators and Accelerators Report showcases twelve incubation programs that deliver exceptionally well on this triple objective.
