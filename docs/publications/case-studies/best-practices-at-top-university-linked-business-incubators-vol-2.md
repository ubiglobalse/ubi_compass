# Best Practices at Top University-linked Business Incubators - Vol 2

## About
Additional 12 International Case Studies of Top-Performing Business Incubators
University-linked business incubators and accelerators continue to show their value and impact in the innovation ecosystems and the broader economy. This second volume of the 2018 Case Studies Report presents twelve additional incubation programs with an equally exceptional performance in the UBI Global World Benchmark Study 2017–2018. These programs exemplify innovation excellence, sustainability, and measurable ecosystem impact.
