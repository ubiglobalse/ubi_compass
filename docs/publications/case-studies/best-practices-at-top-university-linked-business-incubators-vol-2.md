# Best Practices at Top University-linked Business Incubators - Vol 2

## Summary

This second volume adds 12 more international case studies of top-performing university-linked business incubators and accelerators identified through the UBI Global World Benchmark Study 2017-2018.

The report focuses on programs that demonstrate innovation excellence, sustainability, and measurable ecosystem impact, extending the examples introduced in the first case-study volume.
