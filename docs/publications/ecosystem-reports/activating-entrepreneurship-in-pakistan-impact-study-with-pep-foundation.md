# Activating Entrepreneurship in Pakistan - Impact Study with PEP Foundation

## Summary

This UBI Global impact study, produced with the PEP Foundation, examines 31 incubators and accelerators across Pakistan and maps the strengths, constraints, and development potential of the country's emerging innovation ecosystem.

The report positions Pakistan's incubator and accelerator landscape as both a benchmark subject and a policy opportunity, highlighting job creation, youth entrepreneurship, and support for knowledge-based ventures despite constrained resources.

## Publication Details

- Publisher: UBI Global in partnership with PEP Foundation
- Published date: March 2020

- Content type: Ecosystem report / impact study
- Reviewed: 2026-03-29

- Analysis of 31 incubators and accelerators across Pakistan
