# Ahead of the Curve - Impact Study with Ontario Centres of Excellence

## Summary

This impact study examines how Ontario's Campus-Linked Accelerators and On-Campus Entrepreneurship Activities helped strengthen youth entrepreneurship across the province's academic institutions.

Produced by UBI Global with Ontario Centres of Excellence, the report covers 59 accelerators and incubators across 42 institutions and highlights measurable outcomes such as startup support, job creation, and regional economic contribution.

## Publication Details

- Publisher: UBI Global in collaboration with Ontario Centres of Excellence
- Published date: January 2017
- Region: Ontario, Canada
- Content type: Ecosystem report / impact study
- Reviewed: 2026-03-29

## Highlights

- Analysis of 59 accelerators and incubators across 42 academic institutions
- More than 2,200 startups supported, 8,000 jobs created, and $85 million added to Ontario GDP over two years
- A benchmark-backed view of campus entrepreneurship as an economic development engine
- Recommendations relevant to policymakers, universities, and ecosystem leaders
