# Triumph of Innovation - Impact Study with District 2020

## Summary

This impact study documents how incubators, accelerators, and corporate innovation programs around the world adapted during the COVID-19 pandemic and continued supporting startups through disruption.

Based on input from more than 80 organizations across 40 countries, the report highlights operational adaptation, digital transformation, ecosystem resilience, and the practices that helped innovation programs remain effective under pressure.
