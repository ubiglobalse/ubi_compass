# World Rankings Report 2019 - 2020

## About
The UBI Global World Rankings Report 19/20 spotlights the world’s most effective business incubators and accelerators. Based on data from 364 programs across 82 countries , the report showcases how these organizations drive entrepreneurship, create jobs, and power sustainable growth across every continent.
Built on the trusted World Benchmark Study , this edition expands beyond university programs to include public, private, and corporate incubators and accelerators , revealing key trends, success factors, and best practices that define global innovation excellence.
Whether you’re managing an innovation hub or shaping national policy, this report offers a clear view of where the world’s best perform — helping you measure your impact, learn from top performers, and take your ecosystem to the next level. 🌍✨
