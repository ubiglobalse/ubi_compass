# World Rankings Report 2021 - 2022

## About
The UBI Global World Rankings Report 21/22 highlights the most impactful business incubators and accelerators worldwide. Based on data from 109 programs across 56 countries , it reveals how top organizations drive growth, empower startups, and shape innovation ecosystems.
Built on the World Benchmark Study , the report identifies the programs that outperform their peers in value creation, startup success, and ecosystem impact — with special recognition for leaders in ESG excellence .
Explore how the world’s best innovation hubs operate, compare your performance, and be inspired to reach new heights in your own ecosystem. 🌍✨
